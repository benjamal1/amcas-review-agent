# Impactful Experience Essay Rubric

A specific experience that had significant personal impact — NOT the full "why medicine" arc (that's
the personal statement).

**Scored dimensions:** all four shared dimensions from
[`essay-base-rubric.md`](./essay-base-rubric.md) — **Specificity, Reflection Depth, Impact Clarity,
Voice/Authenticity** — plus the overlay below. Score each 1–10 with text-anchored reasoning.

> De-personalized: refer to "the applicant," never a name.

---

## Overlay — Relevance to Medicine

Does the essay connect — explicitly or implicitly — to the decision to pursue medicine or the kind
of physician the applicant wants to be? Does NOT need to be direct; a well-drawn human experience
can earn this implicitly.

| Score | Anchor |
|---|---|
| 1–2 | Experience feels disconnected from the application; no thread to medicine. |
| 3–4 | A medicine tie-in is tacked on at the end, unconnected to the body. |
| 5–6 | Some connection, stated rather than shown. |
| 7–8 | Clear, organic connection to medicine or the applicant's intended practice. |
| 9–10 | The relevance is inseparable from the story — the human insight obviously informs the kind of physician they'll be. |

---

## Scoring checklist
1. Score the 4 base dims + Relevance to Medicine.
2. avg_score = mean of the 5.
3. Write to `data.json` (`component_scores.impactful_experience`) + `feedback/impactful-experience.md`.
4. Note: impactful experience is tracked as its own dashboard tile — **not** folded into the composite.
